Website Creation Services

Why You Need A Website

If you want an online presence, but don't feel like spending your time and energy learning Wix, Squarespace, or other platforms—look no further. Whether you are an individual or a small business, I have the knowledge and experience to take all of the frustration out of creating a simple, yet effective website to advertise your business or personal brand. A website can have a massive impact on your success. In this day and age, people that want to find more information on you or your business are most likely start by searching the web. Therefore, it is essential that you make that information accessible, accurate, and appealing to your target audience.

They can also provide:

Credibility — by increasing new customers' confidence in your legitimacy as a business

Branding — by giving you the opportunity to clearly establish who you are and what you represent

New leads — by providing a place organic traffic to people that stumble upon your sites, as well as for prospective customers to get extra information

Customer service — by reducing some of the workload of staff, increasing your productivity. Many businesses get calls from prospects or existing customers asking simple questions about services, location, and hours of operation

Global communication — by being accessible from anytime, anywhere in the world


The Creative Process

As your web developer, I will work with you to find a vision for your brand or product and then turn that vision into a reality. This process includes:
‣Coming up with a domain name (site address)
‣Choosing a visual design for your website to fit your brand
‣Turning the vision into code
‣Hosting the code on the Internet


Pricing

Everyone enters at a different place in the creative process, and thus has a different set of needs. Maybe you already have a domain and just need a website. Maybe your design is relatively simple and won't require a lot of complex code. That is why my pricing model is an hourly rate of $30, and not a fixed price for a website. Before I begin working on your website, you will receive an estimate of my time. For example, a client that comes in already owning a domain, already know exactly what they want or chooses from a design that I already have, will not require as much time as a client that needs extra attention and fine-tuning.
